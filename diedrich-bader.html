    Diedrich Bader was born on December 24, 1966, in Alexandra, Virginia, USA. His real name is Karl Diedrich Bader. He is of Jewish-Germanic descent. He is the son of Gretta and William B. Bader. Gretta, his Lieutenant’s mother, is a sculptor by trade. She was well-known for her bronze portraiture. From 1991 to 2001, William’s Lt. father was an Assistant Secretary of State for Educational and Cultural Affairs.

    Christopher, Katharine, and John are his three brothers. When he relocated to Paris, he was only two years old. Moving on to his studies, he finished his high school schooling at T.C. Williams High School in Alexandria. After returning to the United States, he attended Groveton High School in Fairfax County. He then attended the University of North Carolina School of the Arts.
