    From Bulbapedia, the community-driven Pokémon encyclopedia.

    Shadow Chill (Japanese: ダークフリーズ Dark Freeze) is one of several Shadow moves available only in Pokémon XD: Gale of Darkness. Like other Shadow moves, it is forgotten and replaced with another move once the Pokémon is purified. It is the signature move of Shadow Articuno.

    Effect

    Shadow Chill is a Shadow version of Ice Beam. It deals damage and has a 10% chance of freezing the target.

    As Shadow Pokémon cannot be traded, and since Pokémon Contests do not exist in Pokémon XD: Gale of Darkness, Shadow Chill cannot be used in Contests.
